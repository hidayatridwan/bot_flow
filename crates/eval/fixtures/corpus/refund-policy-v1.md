# Refund Policy (2023 edition — SUPERSEDED)

This edition was replaced by the 2024 refund policy. It is kept in the corpus on purpose: the point
of the superseded-policy case is that nothing in the retrieval system knows this sentence exists, and
a tenant who uploads a correction without deleting the original has exactly this situation.

## Refund window

Refunds are accepted within 14 days of delivery. After 14 days the sale is final and no refund is
issued, including for unopened goods.

## How to request a refund

Open the order in your account, choose "Request refund", and select a reason. The request is reviewed
within two business days. Approved refunds return to the original payment method.

## Condition of returned goods

Goods must be returned unused and in their original packaging. A restocking fee of 15 percent applies
to any item returned without its packaging.

## Non-refundable items

Gift cards, downloadable software licences, and personalised engraved items are not refundable under
any circumstances.
