# Frequently Asked Questions

A different genre from the handbook on purpose: short question-and-answer pairs, where a fixed-width
chunker is most likely to cut an answer away from its question.

**Can I change the delivery address after ordering?**
Yes, until the order is dispatched. Once you have a tracking code the address is fixed and the parcel
must be redirected through the carrier's own portal.

**Do you offer student discounts?**
We offer 10 percent to verified students through the Studify partner programme. The discount cannot
be combined with seasonal promotions.

**Is there a minimum order value?**
No. There is no minimum, though orders below 75 euros pay the flat shipping fee.

**Can I order by phone?**
No. Telephone support can advise on an order but cannot place one; all orders go through the website.

**Do you sell to businesses?**
Yes. Business accounts get 30 day payment terms after a credit check and are invoiced monthly rather
than per order.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Card, iDEAL, SEPA direct debit, and PayPal. We do not accept cryptocurrency or cheques.

**How do I cancel a subscription?**
Subscriptions are cancelled from the account page and stop at the end of the paid period. We do not
pro-rate a partial month.
