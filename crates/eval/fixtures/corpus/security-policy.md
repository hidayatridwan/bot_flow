# Security and Data Handling Policy

## Account access

Every account requires a password of at least 12 characters. Two-factor authentication is available
by authenticator app; SMS codes were withdrawn in 2023 because of SIM-swap fraud and will not be
reinstated.

A session expires after 30 days of inactivity. Signing out of one device does not end sessions on
other devices; use "Sign out everywhere" from the security page for that.

## What we store

We store the order history, delivery addresses, and the last four digits of a payment card. Full card
numbers are never stored on our systems; they are held by the payment processor and referenced by a
token.

## Data deletion

A deletion request removes the account and its personal data within 30 days. Invoices are exempt:
tax law requires us to retain them for 7 years, so they are retained with the personal fields
redacted rather than deleted outright.

## Breach notification

If a breach affects personal data we notify affected customers within 72 hours of confirming it, and
the supervisory authority within the same period.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Send findings to security@example.test. We do not operate a paid bug bounty, but we do publish an
acknowledgements page and will credit a reporter who asks to be named.
