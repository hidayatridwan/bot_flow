# Refund Policy (2024 edition — CURRENT)

This edition replaces the 2023 refund policy in full. Where the two disagree, this one governs.

## Refund window

Refunds are accepted within 30 days of delivery. The window was extended from the previous 14 days
following customer feedback. Unopened goods may be returned up to 60 days after delivery.

## How to request a refund

Open the order in your account, choose "Request refund", and select a reason. The request is reviewed
within two business days. Approved refunds return to the original payment method.

## Condition of returned goods

Goods must be returned unused and in their original packaging. The restocking fee that applied under
the 2023 policy has been abolished; no fee is charged for a return missing its packaging.

## Non-refundable items

Gift cards, downloadable software licences, and personalised engraved items are not refundable under
any circumstances.
