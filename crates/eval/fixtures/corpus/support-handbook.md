# Northwind Supplies — Customer Support Handbook

## Shipping

Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days within the mainland. Express shipping arrives the next
business day when the order is placed before 14:00. We ship to 24 countries; orders outside the
mainland take 7 to 12 business days and are dispatched from our Rotterdam warehouse.

Shipping is free on orders above 75 euros. Below that threshold a flat fee of 4.95 euros applies,
regardless of weight or destination within the mainland.

## Tracking an order

Every dispatched order receives a tracking code by email within one hour of leaving the warehouse.
The tracking code has the format NW followed by nine digits, for example NW004182337. Codes become
active in the carrier's system roughly four hours after dispatch, so a code that shows no movement on
the first evening is normal and not a sign the parcel is lost.

## Damaged or missing items

Report a damaged item within 48 hours of delivery, with a photograph of the packaging as it arrived.
We replace damaged items at no cost and do not ask for the damaged one to be returned. A missing item
from a multi-item order is resent immediately; we do not wait for the carrier investigation to close.

## Warranty

All hardware carries a 24 month warranty from the date of delivery, covering manufacturing defects.
The warranty does not cover accidental damage, liquid ingress, or wear to consumable parts such as
batteries and cables. A warranty claim needs the order number and a description of the fault.

Batteries are considered consumable and are covered for 6 months only.

## Contacting a human

Support is staffed 08:00 to 18:00 CET on weekdays. Outside those hours the chat assistant can answer
policy questions but cannot alter an order. To reach a person, reply to any order confirmation email
or call +31 20 555 0134. We do not offer support through social media direct messages.
